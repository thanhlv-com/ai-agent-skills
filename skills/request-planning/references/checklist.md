# Request Planning Checklist

## Intake
- Restate user outcome in one sentence.
- Capture constraints: scope, deadline, environment, tools.
- List expected outputs and done criteria.

## Plan Quality
- Steps are ordered and executable.
- Each step has an observable result.
- Dependencies and blockers are identified.
- One active step at a time is clear.

## Risk and Assumptions
- Assumptions are labeled explicitly.
- High-impact unknowns are surfaced early.
- Clarifying question is asked only when truly blocking.

## Validation
- Validation command/check exists for each major change.
- Final summary includes completed checks and residual risks.
- Plan is updated when facts change during execution.
